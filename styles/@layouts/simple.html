Simple layout

Add .layout-simple to the <body> to enable this layout.

Required structure:
Elements marked with * are required

  • body.layout-simple*
    • > header:first-of-type
    • > main:only-of-type
    • > aside:nth-of-type(1), > aside:nth-of-type(2)
      • > nav:only-child
        • > ul
    • > footer:last-of-type
@media (default)
<header>
<main>
<footer>
@media (min-width: 960px)
<header>
<aside>
<main>
<footer>
@media (min-width: 1280px)
<header>
<aside>
<aside>
<main>
<footer>
